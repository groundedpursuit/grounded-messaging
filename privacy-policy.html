Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Grounded Messaging ("the App") is operated by Grounded Pursuit. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information are collected.
  • No conversation data entered in the app is sent to any server.
  • No location data is collected.

2. How the App Works

The App is a self-contained text-based simulator. All processing happens locally on your device. No data leaves your device during normal use.

3. Third-Party Services

The App loads the following third-party libraries from CDN servers for display purposes:

  • Tailwind CSS (styling)
  • Font Awesome (icons)
  • Google Fonts (typography)

These services may log your IP address as part of standard CDN operation. Please review their respective privacy policies for details.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

The App may use your browser's local storage to remember your progress within a session. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact
