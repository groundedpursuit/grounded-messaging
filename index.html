Quick Help

Use this to practice a calm, grounded reply before you send the real text.

  1. Pick a scenario (or tap New Simulation and paste a real message).
  2. Type your reply and hit Send.
  3. Read the feedback + Emotion Decoder, then tap Copy to grab the Grounded Response.
  4. Tap Edit to retry or Continue to keep the conversation going.
  5. Use the gear to switch who you're practicing with + persona.
  6. Use Self-Decode to rewrite what you want to say (emotion + grounded rewrite).
  7. Win a scenario by getting 3 greens in a row, or 2 greens + 1 yellow (no reds) across your last 3 replies.
